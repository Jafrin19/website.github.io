INTRODUCTION

For recent graduates, the transition from college to the workforce is a pivotal moment, particularly for those with B. Tech degree. One pressing question is whether B. Tech graduates find employment in their chosen fields within their local communities after finishing college. This study seeks to address this inquiry by examining retention rates and exploring the factors influencing graduates' career decisions. By leveraging local data, we aim to gain a deeper understanding of employment patterns. The insights gleaned from this research Endeavor will inform targeted strategies for education and workforce development tailored to meet the specific job demands of each region. Ultimately, our goal is to ensure that graduates possess the requisite skills to make meaningful contributions to the economic prosperity of their local areaS.
