Abstract

This abstract provides an overview of the community service project undertaken by students of the Artificial Intelligence department. The project aimed at utilizing AI technologies to address community challenges, demonstrating the practical applications and societal benefits of AI. The project involved a series of activities including data collection, analysis, and implementation of AI solutions, all documented and presented in this report.
