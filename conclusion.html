CONCLUSION

Among the respondents, 64% found success in their B-tech field, while 36% pursued alternative paths due to various challenges. In spite of that, a remarkable 74% expressed satisfaction with their current roles. To assist current B-tech students in making informed career decisions, it is advisable for them to reflect on their interests, explore diverse career opportunities, seek mentorship, and consider future industry trends. Through gaining practical experience, continuous learning, and ensuring alignment with personal values, students can effectively navigate their career paths, fostering long-term fulfillment and success.
